Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a great Indian warrior king and the founder of the Maratha Empire in the 17th century. He is admired for his valour, progressive governance, and unmatched military tactics. His vision, leadership, and unwavering dedication to Swarajya (self-rule) make him one of the most respected leaders in Indian history.

Why I Admire Them

  • He stood against powerful Mughal and foreign forces to establish Swarajya.
  • He believed in inclusive and just governance, respecting all religions and communities.
  • He was a strategic genius and a courageous warrior who fought for people's rights and dignity.
